The spirit of God in a man is not a mere passive quality,but the deliberate preference of right to wrong,the firm and persistent resistance of all moral evil,and the choosing and following of the moral good.

When the spirit takes possession of a person, the spirit is that the character is transformed to reflect the fruit of the spirit which manifest in love,joy,peace,long-suffering,gentleness,goodness, faith,meekness,temperance - Gal 5:22-23.

The spirit-filled life will not struggle with the Holy Spirit but allow him to develop his or her life

  3. Peace
  4. Long-suffering

PREREQUISITE FOR LIVING IN THE SPIRIT

  1. The scripture makes it clear that everyone receives the holy spirit the moment he or she believes in jesus christ as saviour and lord.
  2. The sinful man/old nature produces fruits that reflect that nature and the holy spirit step in at conversion to bring in fruits that reflect the new nature

WHY DO BELIEVERS NEED TO LIVE IN THE SPIRIT

  1. The holy spirit draw the believer to God
  2. Those who walk and live haabituall in the holy spirit,responsive to,controlled and guided by the spirit will not gratify the craving of the flesh
  3. without the holy spirit,no one would seek God

THE MINISTRY OF THE HOLY SPIRIT

  1. He quickens our mortal bodies
  2. He reveals things to come
  3. He reproves the world of sin
CONCLUSION

The primary purpose of the coming of the holy spirit in the life of a believer is to conform us to the image of christ,making the believer more like Jesus.

As teenager experience the fullness of God through the holy spirit and the manifestation of the fruit of the spirit, they will find it easier to show love,kindness,goodness and other fruit with their friends and others.
